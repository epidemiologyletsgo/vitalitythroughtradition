Vitality Through Tradition

Welcome to VTT, nice to meet you!

Who are we?

We are a student run fitness club focused on promoting student health through pilates and traditional Asian fitness techniques.

When do we meet?

We meet every Monday from 6-7pm in Fetzer room 15. Just walk into the SRC and ask a front desk employee if you can't find it!

What do we do?

Our daily meetings consist of a guided workout by one of our trained student instructors focusing on one or two muscle groups. We also do events with other clubs that can be social, volunteering, or fundraising based!
